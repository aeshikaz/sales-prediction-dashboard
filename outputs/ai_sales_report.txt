### 3. Detected Anomalies

Our AI anomaly detection system has flagged specific transactions that deviate significantly from typical patterns, warranting immediate investigation.

**What Was Detected:**
**247 sales transactions** were identified as statistically unusual. These transactions exhibited either exceptionally high sales values or significant financial losses, deviating beyond two standard deviations from the mean.

**Likely Causes:**

*   **Data Accuracy Issues:** Potential data entry errors or system processing anomalies that could inflate sales figures or miscalculate profits.
*   **Unique Business Events:** Large, infrequent sales to key accounts, specific product lines, or unusual scenarios involving substantial discounts or significant product returns impacting profitability.
*   **Potential Fraud:** While less common, extreme outliers can sometimes indicate fraudulent activities.

**Recommended Actions:**

*   **Detailed Transaction Audit:** Conduct an immediate, detailed audit of all 247 flagged transactions. Verify data accuracy, customer details, product information, and associated costs.
*   **Root Cause Investigation:** Systematically investigate the root causes of both significant losses and unusually high sales. This includes scrutinizing discount application, return policies, pricing models, and identifying any unique operational or customer events.
*   **System Review:** Evaluate underlying data entry and processing systems for potential vulnerabilities or areas prone to error.
